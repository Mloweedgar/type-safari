Teacher help · Msaada wa mwalimu

Type Safari teaches keyboard confidence through very short lessons, immediate feedback, rescue practice, games, and passport rewards.

  • Kids follow glowing keys, finish a stop, earn stars, and collect one sticker per stop.
  • Rescue rounds appear when the same key is missed several times, so support stays targeted and simple.
  • The daily mission is only a gentle nudge, not a limit. A child can keep learning long after it is done.
  • The passkey is spa. Use reset current, reset named, or reset all when you need a clean start.
